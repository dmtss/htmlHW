About Our Club

  The Club Deportivo Elemental Sinfín is a tremendously young handball club, founded in April 2004 in the city of Santander. It brings together a management team with a lot of experience, enthusiasm and desire to work to ensure that handball in our city has a representative in the highest category of national handball. This objective was achieved on May 31, 2015 in front of 2,300 spectators who filled the historic Albericia Pavilion in Santander as in the old days. This promotion places the CDE Sinfín in the elite of Spanish handball and after seven years it returns Santander to the real place that by history corresponds to it in this sport. As a reward for the effort in achieving promotion, the entire squad was renewed and, with just three reinforcements, they jumped to ASOBAL with the courage and enthusiasm that characterizes the team. In this maximum category, Rodrigo Reñones's pupils stayed two seasons, after consummating the descent to the Silver Honor Division in May 2017, where he barely resided a year, as the victory in the promotion phase held in June 2018 in Segovia, returned the first team to the national elite. Undoubtedly, teamwork is our greatest strength, at such a difficult time from an economic point of view, where the objectives are to consolidate ourselves in the category and the continued growth of the lower categories. For this we have a large number of national category coaches who, together with the monitors, give total confidence in the daily work with the youth. The success is also due to the institutional support and the sponsors, who have been the key to the growth of our entity.
