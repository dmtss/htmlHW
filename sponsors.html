Sponsors

  The Elemental Sinfín Sports Club is a family in which sponsors and collaborators play a fundamental role. Without them, our project would not be viable and thanks to them, handball is once again exciting in Santander. We have a large number of companies that have been walking hand in hand with us for years and others that have recently decided to bet on a project based on work, sacrifice, humility and hope. Any entity, no matter how small or large, has a place in our family. There are numerous promotion possibilities, both online and offline, and each proposal is treated in a personal way to adjust it to the needs and preferences of each company. If you want to be part of the 'Sinfín Family', please contact us via email sinfin2004@yahoo.es
